FAQ

Do you listen to the demos daily?

Yes. We listen to the demos on a daily basis and leave A&R feedback as much as possible!

How often can I upload a track?

You can upload one track once a week.

Do you automatically sign me and my track once I submit my track?

No. We have an option of 3 months in which we can decide if we want to release it or not. After that you are free to do with your track what you want and aren't bound to anything.
